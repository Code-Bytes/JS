About CodeBytes

CodeBytes is a real time sharing and collaboration platform for the developer community.

Whether you are a seasoned veteran, or just getting started, CodeBytes is a place where you can share short posts, photos, illustrations, videos, screencasts and code snippets.

There is no need to create an account, simply log in with your existing Github account and go to town!

Why Github?
  • No new social media account to maintain
  • Pulling directly from your Github Gists allows you to:
    • Demo
    • Ask for Help
    • Just Plain Show Off
    • And all at the click of a button!
